DIGITAL AUDIO

An Overview of Music

What Is It?

Digital audio is a representaion of sound that is recorded into a digital form. The sound wave of the audio signal is encoded as numerical samples in a continuous sequence.

Formats

The most common way digital audio files are shared is through streaming, such as the platforms shown below:

  • Apple Music
  • Spotify
  • Tidal
  • Pandora

Statistics

Digital Audio may include compression, processing and transmission components. This allows manipulation and transmission. Analog audio results in loss and degredation of audio, however this is not the case with digital audio.

With digital audio, the only way for an error is if audio signals are so large that they confuse the sequence of symbols by being misinterpreted as another symbol. It may be filtered of have effects applied which may tamper the standard sound. It can be stored on:

A CD
A compact disc
A Hard Drive
A data storage device
A Flash Drive
A portable hard drive that can be plugged in and out of a system

Overview

Overall, digital audio can:

  1. Be a direct translation
  2. Be stored on multiple devices
  3. Be tampered with in someway
  4. Not lose it's audio quality over time
